Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about how One Place Site works, who it’s for, and how we recommend destinations.

What is One Place Site?

One Place Site is a curated travel discovery platform that offers mood-based destination suggestions — each page handcrafted for people in a specific life moment, like feeling burned out or blocked.

How do you choose the destinations?

Each destination is selected based on a blend of cultural resonance, logistical ease, emotional compatibility, and travel infrastructure. We prioritize transformative experiences, not trendiness.

Is this site powered by AI?

The content is crafted with care and insight, combining cultural research with travel experience. We do not rely on live AI generation — what you see is fixed and refined for clarity and meaning.

Can I trust the affiliate links?

Yes. We use trusted platforms like Hotellook to offer hotel and travel booking options. Clicking our links supports the site at no extra cost to you.

Will there be more moods or destinations?

Yes. We plan to expand over time based on user feedback, emotional relevance, and global accessibility.

Why is there no blog, newsletter, or account system?

We aim for clarity and speed. The site is intentionally minimal — no tracking, no logins, no distractions. Just one place. For you. Right now.

How can I contact you?

You can reach out via our contact link (coming soon) or message us on our GitHub project page if you have suggestions or feedback.
