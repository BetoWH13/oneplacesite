Tbilisi, Georgia

📍 Coordinates: 41.7151° N, 44.8271° E

✨ Why Here, For You, Now

Tbilisi is where timelines blur and stories intertwine. For the restless, it's a canvas of contradictions: Persian bathhouses beside brutalist monuments, wine bars tucked into medieval courtyards, techno nights fading into Orthodox dawns. This is not a place to arrive — it’s a place to evolve.

🧭 Essentials

  • Best time to go: September to early November
  • Visa: 365-day visa-free access for most countries
  • Etiquette: Toast with intent. Respect hospitality – supra (feast) is sacred.
  • Daily pulse: Morning hikes to Narikala Fortress. Afternoon sulfer baths. Evenings of jazz or heavy beats.
  • More info: r/Georgia – expat & traveler hub

🌀 Hidden Gravity

Tbilisi teaches you to hold chaos with grace. It’s a city with scars and poetry, where old wounds ferment into resilience. For the soul in search of edge, it offers stillness wrapped in volatility.
